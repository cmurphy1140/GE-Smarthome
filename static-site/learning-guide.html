Company Overview

Understanding the powerhouse partnership between GE Smarthome and Savant

1878
GE Smarthome Founded
Thomas Edison establishes the Edison Electric Light Company, which becomes GE Smarthome. Over 140+ years of lighting innovation and expertise.
2005
Savant Systems Founded
Savant emerges as a premium home automation company, focusing on luxury residential control systems and energy management solutions.
2020
Strategic Acquisition
Savant Systems acquires GE Smarthome on July 1, 2020, creating "GE Smarthome, a Savant company" and combining lighting expertise with home automation leadership.
2024
Unified Ecosystem
Launch of the comprehensive dealer program integrating GE Proseo smart home technology with Savant's premium home automation platform.

Technical Specifications

Key technical details dealers need to know for successful installations

📡

Connectivity Options

  • Direct Connect: Works immediately out of box
  • Bluetooth: Local control and pairing
  • 2.4 GHz Wi-Fi: Remote control capabilities
  • Savant Integration: Professional protocol support
⚡

Installation Requirements

  • Neutral Wire: Some switches require, others don't
  • Ground Wire: Required for all switch installations
  • Load Capacity: 600W max for dimmers
  • Compatibility: Works with existing fixtures

Professional Features

  • Scheduling: Advanced time-based controls
  • Scene Setting: Custom home environments
  • Music Sync: Dynamic effects with audio
  • Voice Control: Alexa, Google, Siri support

Installation Note

Always verify home wiring before installation. Homes built before 1980s may lack neutral wires. GE Proseo offers "no neutral wire required" options specifically for these installations. Professional electrical knowledge is recommended for switch installations.
